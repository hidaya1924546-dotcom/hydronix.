About Us

We are a student team developing a smart platform to collect and analyze rainwater instead of wasting it. Our website helps identify flood-prone areas and suggests the best locations for building reservoirs to reuse rainwater sustainably.

Our mission is to reduce flood risks, save water resources, and support cities with data-driven solutions for a safer and more sustainable future.
